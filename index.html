RELAXATION

CREATED BY DEEPAK

Arelaxation technique (also known as Relaxation training) is any method, process, procedure, or activity that helps a person to relax; to attain a state of increased calmness; or otherwise reduce levels of pain, anxiety, stress or anger. Relaxation techniques are often employed as one element of a wider stress management program and can decrease muscle tension, lower the blood pressure and slow heart and breath rates, among other health benefits

Other relaxation techniques may include:
1• Meditation.

2 • Massage.

3• Deep breathing.

3• Music and art therapy.

4• Aromatherapy.

5• Biofeedback.

6• Tai chi.

7• Yoga.
